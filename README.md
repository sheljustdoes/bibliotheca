# bibliotheca

A private library made public.

---

These are the books I return to, the ones I'm working through, and the ones waiting.
No particular genre. A particular kind of curiosity.

The shelf tends toward questions that don't resolve cleanly —
how systems behave, how decisions get made, how intelligence
(human and otherwise) navigates uncertainty.

---

## Themes

<!-- THEMES:START -->
**Prediction & uncertainty**
What can be known in advance, and what can't. How forecasting works when the world resists it.
How reducing the cost of a single cognitive act — prediction — reshapes entire economies.

**Intelligence & decision-making**
The machinery behind judgment. Where intuition holds and where it fails.
How organizations think, and what happens when machines begin to think alongside them.

**Systems & strategy**
The structural forces underneath industries, institutions, and ideas.
What changes when scale, scope, and learning are no longer constrained.

**Identity under pressure**
What persists when the body is replaced, duplicated, or engineered.
Who you are when the system you were built for no longer needs you.
<!-- THEMES:END -->
